Fluffy Golden Retrievers

Golden Retrievers are the ultimate man's best friend. They have the most perfect hair for petting and are the best cuddlers. They are gentle, loyal, and silly.

My Golden Retrievers

I have had two Golden Retrievers as pets. The first was my hunk of love named Trip. After he passed, I had the privilege of loving my best friend, Lance. He sadly passed a few months ago and I miss him dearly.

Golden Retriever Characteristics

  • Temperament: Friendly and outgoing
  • Coat: Dense, water-repellent
  • Size: Medium to large
